# AgilePilot — Warm Intro & Outreach Templates

**Use after:** one-pager ready · NDA template from solicitor · no confidential deep-dives until NDA signed  
**Tone:** partnership, not “please buy our consumer app”  
**Target roles:** partnerships, innovation, product (flexibility / smart), Kraken commercial, developer relations  

---

## 1. Who to contact (target map)

| Organisation | Likely entry points | Notes |
|--------------|---------------------|--------|
| **Octopus Energy** | Partnerships, product (Intelligent Octopus / flexibility), innovation | Primary Path A |
| **Kraken Technologies** | Commercial / licensee success, platform partnerships | OEM for licensees |
| **EDF Retail** | Solutions & automation, open API / developer relations | Multi-provider proof + secondary OEM |
| **Warm intros** | Your network: investors, other founders, energy consultants, lawyers who know energy | Highest conversion |

- “Octopus Energy” + Partnerships / Business Development / Flexibility / Intelligent Octopus  
- “Kraken Technologies” + Partnerships / Product

## 2. Ask a mutual connection for intro (best path)

**Subject / message to introducer:**

```
Hi [Name],

I'm building a white-label load-shifting control plane (multi-tariff adapters +
savings attribution) aimed at energy retailers and Kraken licensees — not a
consumer rates widget.

Would you be comfortable introducing me to anyone in Octopus/Kraken partnerships
or flexibility product? Happy to send a one-pager first so you can decide if it's
a fit. Mutual NDA before any deep technical discussion.

Thanks,
William
Capstone Software Solutions
```

**Forwardable blurb (for them to paste):**

```
William Massey at Capstone builds a multi-tariff load-shifting control plane
that energy brands can white-label (adapters for open tariffs, savings ledger,
multi-tenant SaaS). They're exploring a paid pilot / OEM path with retailers
and Kraken-family platforms — not competing with billing. One-pager available;
happy to NDA for a technical deep-dive.
```

## 4. Cold email (after connection or public address)

**Subject lines (pick one):**

1. White-label load-shifting for Kraken licensees — pilot conversation  
2. Multi-tariff control plane (not another rates widget)  
3. Intro: Capstone × [Octopus/Kraken] flexibility software  

**Body:**

```
Hi [First name],

I'm William Massey, founder of Capstone Software Solutions (UK).

We're building a multi-tariff load-shifting control plane that retailers can
white-label: normalised half-hourly rates across open supplier APIs, shift
policies, and an auditable savings ledger — sitting beside billing, not inside it.

Why it might matter to [Octopus / Kraken]:
• Device and multi-tariff orchestration is ongoing integration debt for platform teams
• Kraken-family open tariff APIs (including EDF's open residential APIs) make a
  multi-provider control plane useful for licensees, not only one brand
• We package for enterprise evaluation (NDA, security baseline, paid pilot SOW)

Happy to send a one-page overview. If there's interest, we'd sign a mutual NDA
before any architecture deep-dive or pilot scoping.

Would a 20-minute call in the next fortnight work?

Best regards,
William Massey
Capstone Software Solutions
[email] | [phone] | [website]
```

## 6. Post-NDA meeting agenda (20–30 min)

1. Their goals: flexibility, licensee differentiation, device coverage  
2. What we are / are not (not replacing Kraken/billing)  
3. Demo: multi-provider slots API + enterprise mode (public rates)  
4. Security & data minimisation summary  
5. Pilot shape: 8–12 weeks, paid, success criteria  
6. Next: SOW draft or polite pass  

---

## 7. What never to say

| Avoid | Prefer |
|-------|--------|
| “We show Octopus Agile better than your app” | “We white-label load-shift orchestration for devices and multi-tariff policies” |
| “Affiliate referrals to switch to Octopus” as the pitch | OEM / licensee value |
| “We’ll give you the source if you try it free” | Paid pilot + licence |
| Detailed architecture pre-NDA | One-pager + high-level only |
| Disparaging Intelligent Octopus | Complementary to existing smart products |
